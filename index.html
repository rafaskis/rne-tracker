Managing Recurring Cycles

When updating or removing item sequences tied to continuous intervals, a distinct selection prompt will guide your target action scope:

  • "Current" (On the Left Side): Focuses changes exclusively to your active display month.
  • "All" (On the Right Side): Sweeps changes entirely across all matching future cycles down the series roadmap.
